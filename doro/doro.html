ethiopian traditional food doro wat

descripion

doro wat is ethiopian traditional food. it is a delicious food made from the chicken

ingredient

  • chicken
  • water
  • pepper
  • oil
  • egg
  • salt
  • other recipes

steps to do

  1. boil water
  2. wash the chicken meat
  3. mix the pepper,salt and other recipe and cook it
  4. boil the egg
  5. add the meat and the egg in the cooker
